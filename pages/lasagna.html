Lasagna

Description

A recipe perfected over the ages!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cooke the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
